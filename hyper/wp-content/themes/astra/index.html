We straddle the line between virtual and real, exploiting the potential of 3D to break out of the classic patterns of thought. We are not digital natives but we have made digital our workhorse, embracing the creative freedom to produce solutions that connect, communicate, and inspire.

HyperLab exists in three dimensions, where exploits all its multi-disciplinary capabilities to realize unique projects. The fil rouge that distinguishes us and connects everything we realize is 3D, which for HyperLab is not only a tool but a real modus operandi.

  • Digital Architecture

  • Virtual Products

01

Concept

Whether it is a building or a piece of furniture, a preliminary project or an advanced one, we design it and present it through photorealistic 3d imagery and explanatory graphic content.

02

Photorealistic images

based on a photographic approach. We use the latest visualisation technology to describe any type of project or product. The flexibility of virtual production in lighting, enviroment creation, manipulation of every single detail, makes this narrative tool central in our workflow.

03

Animations

Photorealistic 3d animation can increase the communications possibilities of any projects. Today the latest technologies make it possible to create amazing animations with cinematographic quality.

04

Augmented experiences

Virtual experiences take exploration and communication to a new immersive level. With the possibility of enrich them with pop-up infographics, floorplans, videos, links, they represent a very complete and smart way to describe many type of projects.
